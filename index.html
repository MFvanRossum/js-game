The "Pew-Pew" Square

So you woke up and you're a square. Well, this isn't ideal.

But what's this? "Pew! Pew!"

You can fire lazers. How about that.

And not a moment too soon. Here come some nasty rectangles. And they don't seem to like you. Best get after it!

Use the LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys to move.

Fire your "pew-pew" lazer with SPACE.

If you die, don't worry about it! Just reload the page to try again!
